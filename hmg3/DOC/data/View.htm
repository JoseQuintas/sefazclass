View Property
Specifies the initial view of a MonthCalendar control

 

 

  

Syntax:

 

View <nValue> (Control Definition)

 

<WindowName>.<ControlName>.View := nValue

 

<WindowName>.<ControlName>.View --> nValue

 

nValue must be one of the following.

 

Constant ValueMeaning
MCMV_MONTH 0 Monthly view
MCMV_YEAR 1 Annual view
MCMV_DECADE 2 Decade view
MCMV_CENTURY3 Century view

 

Remarks:

 

In the control, the view can be changed by clicking on the title.
